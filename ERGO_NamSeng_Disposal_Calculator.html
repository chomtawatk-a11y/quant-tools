Donation Scenario — Why It Is Excluded
Thai Revenue Code Section 65 tri — 2% cap on net profit
Donation deduction is capped at 2% of net profit — NOT 2× BV as initially assumed. At ฿1,000M profit, only ฿20M is deductible. Remaining ฿580M is added back → taxable income = ฿1,580M → tax = ฿316M (extra ฿116M vs not donating). Donation is EXCLUDED from main analysis. Confirm qualifying recipient eligibility with tax advisor.
